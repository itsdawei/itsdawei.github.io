CV

Currently applying to Ph.D. for Fall 2025!

I am a master's student in computer science at University of Southern California (USC), where I also obtained my bachelor's degree in computer science with honors. I am a member of the Interactive and Collaborative Autonomous Robotics (ICAROS) lab @ USC, where I am fortunate to be advised by the wonderful Stefanos Nikolaidis and mentored by Bryon Tjanaka. I am also honored to have worked closely with David Kempe and Shaddin Dughmi.

I am interested in continuous optimization, interpretability in machine learning, robust artifical intelligence, and quality diversity optimization. I am excited about deriving practically useful algorithms from theoretical computer science.

I am committed to utilizing my skills in software engineering and problem solving to making positive impacts on people's life.
